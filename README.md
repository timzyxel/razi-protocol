# Razi Protocol

Razi Protocol is a massive progression and compatibility layer for Factorio: Space Age, built to make a huge planet-focused modpack feel like one connected journey instead of a pile of separate destinations.

It reshapes the galaxy into a longer, more deliberate route through multiple star systems, custom planet tiers, hostile biomes, system science cards, and a deeper Krastorio 2 Spaced Out endgame. The goal is simple: every planet should have a reason to exist, every science pack should matter, and the road to deep space should feel earned.

## What This Mod Does

- Rebuilds the space map around custom systems like Solaris, Nyxaris, Vibrant, Beetlejuice, and Deep Space.
- Integrates many planet mods into a shared progression path instead of leaving them as disconnected side content.
- Adds system tech cards that condense huge science requirements into cleaner system-level milestones.
- Reworks planet discovery techs so later systems depend on the science and discoveries from earlier systems.
- Creates a custom deep-space endgame around Nexus, Void Processing, Sol, the intergalactic transceiver, and a final singularity-style progression.
- Routes modded enemies onto planets that fit them better, while reducing default enemy spam on worlds meant to have custom threats.
- Adds compatibility fixes for several K2SO, planet, science, enemy, and quality-of-life mods.
- Keeps credits and compatibility notes explicit so the original mod authors remain properly acknowledged.

## Progression Vision

Razi Protocol treats the modpack like a staged expedition.

The early game begins in the familiar Calidus region with the base Space Age loop. From there, the player branches into Solaris and the nearby planet networks, then pushes deeper into Nyxaris and Dea Dia style content, then onward into Vibrant, Beetlejuice, and finally Deep Space.

Each system is intended to become its own chapter. You explore its planets, unlock their science, combine that science into a system card, and use those cards to prove you are ready for the next leap.

By the time the player reaches Nexus, Sol, and the late-game transceiver chain, the expectation is that the factory has mastered nearly everything before it.

## Systems And Themes

- Calidus: The base game and Space Age foundation, eventually represented by its own system card.
- Solaris: The first major custom system layer, tying together several planet and moon discoveries.
- Nyxaris: A deeper progression tier that now pulls Dea Dia into the same broader stage.
- Vibrant: A strange, high-energy system for exotic and dangerous planets.
- Beetlejuice: A harsh late-game system with planets such as Cubium, Ribbonia, Crucible, Vesta, and Aquilo.
- Deep Space: The far end of the route, focused on Nexus, Void Processing, Sol, black holes, and transceiver-gated endgame goals.

## Modpack Role

This is not meant to be a tiny standalone content mod. Razi Protocol is built as glue, structure, and balance for a very large Space Age modpack.

It depends on many planet and enemy mods and intentionally conflicts with a few mods that try to solve the same endgame space. If something looks opinionated, it probably is. The mod is trying to turn chaos into a coherent adventure.
