Razi Protocol Credits and Acknowledgements
Razi Protocol is a compatibility, progression, and system-layout mod built around a large collection of Factorio 2.0 and Space Age mods. 
Credit for the original planets, enemies, systems, mechanics, art, sounds, icons, and ideas belongs to the authors and contributors of those mods.
This project exists because of the work of the Factorio modding community. 
If you are the creator of a mod or asset referenced here and want credit adjusted, wording changed, or something removed, please contact me on Discord: Raziphel.


Core foundation
- Wube Software for Factorio and Space Age.
- PlanetsLib for making large custom planet and system setups practical.
- Krastorio2 Spaced Out for the overhaul foundation that this modpack progression is built around.

Endgame inspiration
Razi Protocol's late-game transceiver, deep-space card, Nexus, Sol, and singularity progression was also inspired by the general idea of extending Krastorio's endgame beyond the normal finish line.
Special acknowledgement to the Krastorio2 Extended Endgame / K2 Spaced Out Extended Endgame lineage and authors for that style of late-game concept. 
Razi Protocol does not depend on those mods directly and intentionally conflicts with them so its own endgame route can be used instead.
